My Process

When creating a website for you, I split the process into stages to offer a complete service. I start by understanding your needs and creating a tailored design, I then bring it to life through clean, efficient code. Once developed, I handle everything from testing to hosting, ensuring a seamless launch of your website. Let's work together to create a site that meets your goals and engages your customers.

Design and Planning

To begin, we will have a detailed discussion to gain a clear understanding of your goals for the website and the desired visual style. This ensures we are aligned and set a strong foundation for the project moving forward.

  • I want to know your ideas, your audience & your market. Understanding what you want is most important
  • Drafting: I will create sample designs and listen to your feedback to mold the site into what you vision
  • Branding: Decorating your site with appropriate colours and fonts to match your brand perfectly

Development

Once you approve the design, I’ll start coding and developing the website. This process turns the design into a fully functional, interactive site. I’ll ensure it matches the design while building a responsive, efficient backend that supports the required features.

  • I will develop the front end using modern technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a visually appealing, responsive website that maintains high performance and follows best practices
  • I will set up the website's backend to manage the underlying logic, ensuring it delivers the functionality you need

Implementation

Finally, I will prepare the website for launch, making it ready for everyone to see. If desired, I can also manage your domain and hosting to ensure a smooth setup and easy maintenance.

  • I will test the website to catch any bugs and ensure it works as required on a wide range of devices, browsers and screen sizes
  • I'll upload the site to a hosting server and connect your chosen domain to link them together.
  • I can provide ongoing support and maintenance, addressing any issues, performing updates, and making content changes to ensure your site remains up-to-date and visually appealing.
